Data Provenance

These datasets were extracted from Wikidata using SPARQL queries and supplemented with web-sourced editorial records. Authority identifiers (IsisCB, VIAF) were matched using a combination of VIAF ID reconciliation and name-based matching. The data represents a snapshot and may not include all scholars active in the field. For corrections or additions, please contribute to Wikidata or contact IsisCB.
